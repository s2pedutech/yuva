Yuva

Yuva Foundation is an NGO established to promote youth empowerment and welfare activities, aimed at the alleviation of human anguish and overall enrichment of the society and youth, who will be representatives of the future of the nation, Yuva Foundation is also geared to refine the youth, by imparting students with education, training and other skills by conducting youth empowerment programmes that give them insight of the opportunities of government and private sector. Above all, they are constantly encouraged to focus on education and other such trainings as this is the major key for attaining meaningful heights in life. Our Mission is to work on - “Overall Student Fraternity Career wise & support to the development of Youth”.
